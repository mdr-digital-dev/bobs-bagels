How It's Made

The old-school way.
Every time.

1
Mix the dough
High-gluten flour, malt, water. No softeners. Mixed by hand.
2
Hand-roll each one
Every single bagel shaped by hand. You can taste the difference.
3
Cold ferment overnight
12–18 hours in the fridge. Slow ferment = that chewy, complex flavor.
4
Kettle boil & bake
Boiled in honey-malt water, then hot oven. That's where the crust comes from.

About Bob

He's been doing this a while.

Bob didn't set out to make the best bagels in Chicago. He just couldn't find one he liked, so he learned to make them himself. That was twelve years ago.

What started as a Saturday thing for friends became a farmers market stand, then this. Still hand-rolled. Still kettle-boiled. Still no shortcuts.
