How It's Made

The old-school way.
Every time.

1
Mix the dough
High-gluten flour, malt, water. No softeners, no shortcuts. Mixed by hand.
2
Hand-roll each one
Every bagel shaped by hand. You can tell the difference. Trust us.
3
Cold ferment overnight
Into the fridge for 12–18 hours. Slow fermentation = that chewy, complex flavor.
4
Kettle boil & bake
Boiled in honey-malt water, then straight into a hot oven. That crust? That's why.

About Bob

He's been doing this a while.

Bob didn't set out to make the best bagels in Chicago. He just couldn't find one he liked, so he learned to make them himself. That was twelve years ago.

What started as a Saturday morning thing for friends turned into a farmers market stand, which turned into this. Still hand-rolled. Still kettle-boiled. Still no shortcuts.
